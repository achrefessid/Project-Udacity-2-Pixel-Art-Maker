Leaf Cutter Ants

Next to humans, leaf cutter ants form the largest and most complex animal societies on earth.

  • Number of Species: 47
  • Geographic Home: Mexico, Centarl and South America
  • Largest Colony Size: 10 million
  • Strength: Leafcutter ants can carry more than three times their body weight

Leafcutter ants gather leaves not as food-source, but as a tool to harvest fungi which is what the colony eats. A leafcutter ant colony consists of many roles and are said to be teh next most complex animal on earth next to humans.
